About Us

Our mission at Consolidated Holdings Group (CHG), also known as the Conings Group, is to build and maintain a diversified capital platform focused on long-term value creation through disciplined investment, asset management, and strategic ownership positions. We are a privately held investment and holdings group with global interests across operating companies, public and private markets, structured assets, and strategic investment positions. Our approach is centered on long-term capital allocation, asset management, and portfolio construction, with a focus on acquiring, holding, and developing investments across select sectors and markets.

Our Approach

Our approach is grounded in disciplined, long-term capital allocation across a diversified range of investment opportunities and ownership positions. We evaluate opportunities through a framework that considers structural market trends, sectoral transformation, and long-term value creation potential. Investment decisions are guided by a focus on capital preservation, risk-adjusted exposure, and sustainable growth over extended time horizons. CHG maintains flexibility across asset classes and investment structures, including operating companies, private investments, public market positions, and structured assets. This flexibility enables the group to deploy capital selectively across both established and emerging sectors. Our portfolio construction process prioritises balance between stability and growth, with an emphasis on acquiring, holding, and developing positions that demonstrate durable long-term strategic value.

Portfolio

CHG maintains a private diversified portfolio of long-term holdings and investment positions across operating companies, private market investments, public market exposures, and structured assets. The portfolio is constructed to balance stability with selective exposure to long-term growth opportunities across global sectors and regions. Each position is evaluated within a long-duration framework, with emphasis on strategic alignment, capital efficiency, and resilience across market cycles. Portfolio composition reflects a disciplined approach to capital allocation, with an emphasis on acquiring, holding, and developing positions that demonstrate durable strategic value over time.
